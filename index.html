Why Kompost?

Composting isn't just about reducing trash. It's about participating in the natural cycle of life.

Reduce Waste

Divert organic scraps from landfills where they would otherwise release harmful methane gas.

Enrich Soil

Create "black gold" that retains moisture and suppresses plant diseases and pests naturally.

Save Money

Reduce the need for commercial fertilizers and lower your trash collection footprint.

Simple Steps to Start

You don't need a huge garden. Whether you have a backyard or a small apartment, you can start today.

1

Choose Your Bin

Select a spot in your yard or get a sealed under-sink bin for indoors.

2

Add Greens & Browns

Mix vegetable scraps (Greens) with dry leaves or paper (Browns) for balance.

3

Wait & Aerate

Turn the pile occasionally to add oxygen. In a few months, you'll have soil!
